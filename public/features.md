# OpsRelic Features & Product Guide

This document outlines the detailed operations and capabilities of the OpsRelic platform. 

## Dashboard & Agency Overview
- View aggregated metrics across all your active campaigns.
- See real-time submission activity and recent clip links uploaded by the team.
- Track overall views, engagement rates, and ROI across multiple client portfolios.

## Pipeline & Client Management
- Manage different clients and their associated campaigns in a kanban-style pipeline.
- Access client-specific resources, brand colors, and assets.
- Drill down into specific campaigns to manage briefs, submissions, and metrics.

## Campaign Tracking & Analytics
- Create new campaigns under specific clients.
- Add clip links from supported platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts).
- The system automatically parses these links and fetches real-time public metrics (Views, Likes, Comments) to showcase performance.
- Review campaign performance through integrated charts (Views over time, engagement distribution).

## Client Portal
- Generate secure, read-only portals for your clients.
- Provide a branded experience where clients can securely log in to see their campaign's performance without having access to your internal tools.
- Portals update in real-time as your team adds new metrics and clips.

## Settings & Workspace Management
- Customize workspace branding (colors, logos) to match your agency's identity.
- Manage billing and subscription tiers (Starter, Pro, Agency) integrated via Whop.
- Configure notification preferences and team members.

## Security & Architecture
- Workspace-isolated data architecture ensures that you never see data from other agencies, and your clients never see data from other campaigns.
- Real-time database synchronisation means your dashboards are always up to date the second a new clip is logged.
