# OpsRelic - Landing Page

**The Operating System for Clipping Agency & UGC Teams.**

Centralize your agency operations. From raw performance metrics to branded client portals, OpsRelic v1 provides the infrastructure for professional clipping and UGC teams to report fast, stay organized, and scale efficiently.

## Core Capabilities: The OpsRelic Suite
One Operating System for your entire agency. From intake to final portal delivery, manage every aspect of your agency inside OpsRelic.

- **Agency Home & Operations**: A command center for your entire agency. Get real-time alerts, monitor team activity, and manage workspace settings all in one view.
- **Client & Campaign Management**: Store campaign briefs, asset libraries, targets, and deliverable status. Keep everything related to a client organized.
- **Real-time Agency Analytics**: Automatically consolidate performance data across campaigns. Track views, engagement, and KPIs with enterprise precision.
- **Branded Client Portals**: Secure, client-facing workspaces. Deliver live-updated performance reports without ever sending another PDF deck.
- **Centralized Brand Assets**: A unified library for agency-wide brand assets, logos, and campaign templates for quick access.
- **Streamlined Intake & Workflow**: Stop managing campaign updates via email. Use standardized intake and automated status updates to align the team.

## Coming in v2: The Campaign Wizard
Launch a campaign in 10 minutes, not 10 emails. Brief in, target KPIs in, OpsRelic spits out a structured campaign workspace with templates, reporting views, and "what works" insight packs. Founding-tier customers get early access.
